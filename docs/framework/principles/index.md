# Architecture Principles

## 1. Business Before Technology

Technology exists to enable business value.

---

## 2. API First

Every capability should be exposed through managed APIs.

---

## 3. Cloud Native by Default

Prefer managed cloud services whenever possible.

---

## 4. Security by Design

Security is integrated from the beginning.

---

## 5. Event Driven

Systems communicate asynchronously whenever appropriate.

---

## 6. Automation First

Everything repeatable should be automated.

---

## 7. Observability by Default

Every platform must expose logs, metrics and traces.

---

## 8. Reuse Before Build

Reuse enterprise capabilities before developing new ones.

---

## 9. Data as a Product

Treat data as a strategic asset.

---

## 10. AI Ready

Architectures must be designed to integrate AI capabilities securely.
